UIUX Website Profil

I have designed and developed the interface (UI) and user experience (UX) for my portfolio website. To do this, I used Figma, a powerful digital design tool. With Figma, I can create every page view in detail, including layout, colors, design elements, and interactions. This allows me to design and visualize every aspect of my portfolio website before implementing it as an actual website. Figma helps me gain a better understanding of how visitors will interact with my website and ensure it looks professional and attractive. This is my website plan that I have designed using the Figma application. I follow UI/UX principles to ensure that users who view my website will feel comfortable. Thus, when this design is implemented with code, users will have a better experience.

Technology Used : Figma
